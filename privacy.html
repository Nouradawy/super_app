Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the WhatsUnity app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by nouradawy (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Freemium service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information includes:

  • Personal Identification: Full Name, Username, Phone Number, Email Address.
  • Residency Information: Owner Type (Owner/Rental), Apartment Number, Building Number, and Compound ID.
  • Media: Profile pictures and other media uploaded by you.
  • Device Information: Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address), operating system, and device identifiers.

Verification Documents & Proof of Ownership

To ensure the security of the community and resolve ownership conflicts (e.g., if two users claim the same unit), the Service Provider may request you to upload Proof of Ownership documentation (such as a utility bill, contract, or deed).

  • Storage: These documents are securely stored in our database (Supabase) in a restricted storage bucket.
  • Access: These documents are accessible only by authorized administrators for verification purposes.
  • Purpose: The data is used strictly to verify your residency status and resolve conflicts. It is not shared with third-party advertisers.

Location Data

The Application collects your device's location to provide features such as personalized content and relevant community recommendations. This data also helps analyze user behavior to improve the Application.

Google Drive Access

The Application allows you to share images and voice notes within the community chat. To facilitate this:

  • The Application requests access to your personal Google Drive storage.
  • This access is used solely to upload media files (images/audio) generated by you within the Application.
  • The Application creates and views only the files it uses; it does not scan or modify your other personal files.
