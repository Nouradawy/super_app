Request Account Deletion

To request the deletion of your WhatsUnity account and all associated data:

  1. Send an email to support@whatsunity.work.gd with the subject "Delete My Account".
  2. Please send this email from the email address associated with your account so we can verify ownership.
  3. We will process your request and permanently delete your data (including profile information, files, and chat history) within 30 days.
